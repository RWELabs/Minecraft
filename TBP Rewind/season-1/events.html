The Events of Season One



The 2019 Halloween Event

The Baked Potatarium Halloween Event for 2019 (Nicknamed Halloween Town) was the first ever seasonal event held on the server. A trailer was released to announce the Halloween Event on September 29, 2019. The event officially began on October 1st and was held in a swamp biome between the Mewffin Isles and the Crutionix Public Use Guardian Farm. The trailer features the original Halloween Town setup before being opened to the public, as prepared by OnyonSwomp and Crutionix. Halloween Town featured several 3D pixel arts, including a giant pumpkin and skull, along with custom trees and a slime wearing a witches hat.

The event was advertised to include activities for server members to enjoy including a build competition that would last the entire month of October and an event where the time of day would be halted at dusk, to be held on October 31st. Special decorative heads were also advertised as being available for a limited time during the event. This event marks the first time that users could teleport to an open, accessible space. Previously teleports were reserved for in-game user polling, which only provides a teleport in and out of the polling location.

The 2019 Christmas Event

The Baked Potatarium Christmas/Holiday Event for 2019 (Nicknamed Holiday Hills) was the second seasonal event to take place on the server. The event follows the Halloween Event (2019). The event included activities, shopping opportunities, a secret Santa event, opportunities to build and also a snow-globe decoration competition. The event officially began on December 1, 2019 and ran through until the New Year.

The Funeral of Green Sheep
Green Sheep is a sheep that was originally one of the many coloured sheep located in the pens at Solanum during Season 1 of The Baked Potatarium. It is unknown how Green Sheep died, but his remains were found on the ground in spawn chunks on June 9, 2019 at 10:33pm. He has been buried next to the Solanum Museum where his remains were found. The headstone is made from the wool and the mutton that was dropped has been buried in a chest.



The Grief of 2019
The Grief was a server-wide event that took place mostly at Solanum (known then as Spawn) as well as some player's bases. It occurred on April 26, 2019 and lead to the server adding a whitelist to prevent players from adding unless they requested to play. The Grief involved many buildings being destroyed, partially destroyed or damaged. It was the first time the server was rolled back to a backup save and the only time that the server was rolled back to a backup save outside of moderation testing for game updates and features.

The Grief was instigated by two members that were given the IP from someone inside of The Nutty Terrarium Discord server, when the server was public for anyone to play within the Discord. The players were later identified as being friends of aHoboAteMyDog.

The Grief took place on April 26, 2019 between 10:00am and 2:46pm. The destruction was documented by several players in the form of screenshots. The notable buildings that were griefed included:

The Solanum Hotel
H4v0c_x_S4nTA and meagansan2000's House
The Spawn Museum
Glowe6958's House
FriedOrange79's starter base.



The Battle of Solanum Fields
The Battle of Solanum Fields (also known as The Great Pillager Raid) was an event of conflict that lasted for a week from start to finish in the week of June 28 to July 5 of 2019 on The Baked Potatarium. It is unsure who instigated the Battle of Solanum Fields, however it is likely that Portugal officially began the conflict as a reaction to the increasing threat of military pillagers gathering close to the city.

The battle was an overwhelming loss for the Pillager Military, losing every available troop for battle. Portugal did not lose a single civilian or military troop during the war due to the advanced armour and medical items at their disposal. The Portuguese Military consisted of the leader of Portugal, Eternaldoom, fusionedge, FriedOrange79 and the leader of Ruleskeid, Crutionix.

Pre-battle Events
The Battle of Solanum was preceded by a growing threat on the borders of Portugal's military zone when civilian transport noticed the gathering of many different pillager patrols that seemed to be facing in the direction of the Town Centre of Portugal. It was considered a humorous occurrence for some time between June 6 to 16, 2019 until the threat had grown to a state too large to be handled by the Portuguese military alone.

The threat continued to grow between June 16 until June 28 when plans for defense began forming after FriedOrange79 elevated concerns about the growing threat.

Ruleskeid Response
The nearby village of Ruleskeid also experienced threats from the Pillager Military however it was on a much smaller scale. As a result of this threat, walls were constructed to the western side of Ruleskeid, to protect the village from any attacks and limit entry into the village from just two points along the western border. The wall proved to be effective and no conflict was raised after it was constructed.

As a result of this threat, the leader of Ruleskeid, Crutionix, pledged assistance to Portugal in the event of any conflict with the Pillager military.

Course of the Battle
The first casualty marked the official start of the battle. The first pillager militia was slain on July 1, 2019. Over the following hours, many other pillagers were slain by eternaldoom, fusionedge and FriedOrange79. Further to the North-East of the battle there were more pillagers that seemed to be drifting in the direction of Portugal. These were closer to Ruleskeid and were taken care of by Crutionix. Over the following days, more pillager militia were discovered and slain accordingly.

The battle was officially declared a victory for Portugal on July 5, 2019 when there were little to no pillagers found in the area.

Aftermath
While the battle was a victory for Portugal, Pillager military patrols still tend to make their presence known at the border of Portugal's military zone. Despite this, a threat has not been considered. The city of Portugal went on to found the Feitoria and hold an event where local wines were served to visitors. There were no Pillagers present at this celebratory event.

The Marriage of Mewffin and Crutionix
The Marriage of Crutionix and Mewffin is the first and only recorded marriage during Season 1 of The Baked Potatarium. It is also the first and only service to take part at the Church of Robsoni. The marriage (namely the event) caused controversy as the event was originally supposed to unite meagansan2000 and H4v0c_x_S4nta until the bride fled during the event.

Ceremony
The ceremony commenced at at 1:00am on May 4, 2019 and was originally intended to marry meagansan2000 and H4v0c_x_S4nta. Originally, Mewffin was the flower girl and Crutionix was wedding photographer. This can be seen in photographs taken by Crutionix to celebrate the wedding.

The ceremony of Mewffin and Crutionix specifically commenced at 1:07am. The wedding procedure started with Robsoni introducing the marriage and giving a short speech about marriage, the couple and the importance of marriage. Vows were exchanged before the couple were married. The couple were showered in baked potatoes and a music disc was played.

This was the only marriage event to occur in Season One, but would set a precedent for future seasons - as every season to follow (with the exception of Skyblock) would have a marriage event.

Guests
Guests that were in attendance during the wedding include:

  • Crutionix (Wife)
  • Mewffin (Husband)
  • meagansan2000
  • H4v0c_x_S4nTA
  • Robsoni (Reverend)
  • OnyonSwomp (Organ Player)
  • FriedOrange79
  • Glowe6958

Post Wedding Celebrations
After the wedding, most of the guests retreated to Orange Hill where post-wedding celebrations took place. A large heart made out of wool was constructed at the site and fireworks were deployed.



The Sharing of Alex's D*k
Alex's D*k was a renamed enchanted Diamond Sword with 582 durability remaining. It had been in possession by several members on the server. It's final position was being stored at H4v0c_x_S4nTA and meagansan2000's house, where it was being used in an armour stand diorama depicting the death of UwU, as a part of the Uwu-Cleanse of 2019. The photo depicts Alex's D*k in an item frame at Crutionix's starter home. Crutionix possessed the item the longest of anyone other than H4v0c21_x_S4nTA and meagansan2000.



The UwU Cleanse of 2019
The UwU Cleanse of 2019 was an impromptu event in which the bible of UwU was officially destroyed. UwU was removed by moderators from the server, sparking the cleanse. An armour stand Diorama was constructed by meagansan2000 and H4v0c_x_S4nTA depicting the demise of UwU.

The event lasted several months and involved all traces of the player removed from the server. This includes the destruction of player heads belonging to UwU. The main bible burning event occurred on August 12, 2019 and took place at the starter base of FriedOrange at Orange Hill. The original copy of the Bible of UwU was burned by H4v0c_x_S4nta followed by copies of the bible. A single copy of the Bible of UwU is available in the Solanum Museum along with several Burnt Pages.



The Kidnapping of Reginald
Reginald was a chicken that was roaming around near Crutionix's starter base in Ruleskeid. Reginald wandered into the space between the Nether Portal and a cobble stone wall when he progressed to adulthood, making him unable to move due to the increase in hitbox size. It is unknown what the chances are of this happening.

He remained in this location until he was stolen by a removed member of the server. He was later recovered and moved to the Mewffin Isles where he resided for some time before being finally relocated to a secret location by Crutionix.

Reginald was named after FriedOrange79's nickname in Facebook Messenger with Crutionix. The name tag was supplied by FriedOrange. 

Reginald produced many eggs in his time standing between the portal and wall and many of those were hatched. It is unknown how many siblings he has. Reginald the Second is a chicken that was slain by FriedOrange79 at the Age of Empires base by eternaldoom however there doesn't seem to be a relation between the two. He is quite the iconic member of the server, being featured in many streams.
